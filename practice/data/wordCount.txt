Machine learning has become an integral part of modern data science, enabling models to learn from vast amounts of information. 
By utilizing supervised and unsupervised learning techniques, businesses can predict customer behavior, optimize supply chain operations, and even diagnose diseases. 
Deep learning, a subset of machine learning, relies on neural networks to process complex data patterns, enhancing accuracy in image recognition and natural language processing tasks. 
The growing availability of cloud computing and big data technologies further accelerates the adoption of machine learning across various industries.
